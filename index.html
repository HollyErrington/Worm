I like worms

Have you every wondered about worms? I sure have. Their wriggly aura penetrates my mind every night before I enter the realm of sleep. I think I may have been a worm in a past life. As Siddhartha Gautama said, "Worms are the most moral of creatures". I am a moral creature - I even studied philosophy at Oxford, therefore I have a lot in common with worms. There are at least 20,000 species of worm. The only worms I do not like are peanut worms because they look like peanuts and I believe that peanuts should be banned. The reason you should like worms is because I like worms and I am very authoritative. My flatmate once called me "confrontational". Today I am confronting you about worms. I would happily put a worm in my mouth. They are very friendly. I think they should have voting rights.
